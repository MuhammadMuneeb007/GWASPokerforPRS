


This folder contains 3 asthma GWAS summary statistics from UK Biobank: 1. all asthma, 2. childhood-onset asthma, 3. adult-onset asthma. For detailed phenotyping and QC procedure, please refer to our paper "Shared Genetics of Asthma and Mental Health Disorders: A Large-Scale Genome-Wide Cross-Trait Analysis. European Respiratory Journal, 2019 (PMID: 31619474)"


ukb.allasthma.upload.final.assoc (case N=46802, control N=347481)
ukb.childasthma.upload.final.assoc (case N=9676, control N=347481)
ukb.adultasthma.upload.final.assoc (case N=22296, control N=347481)

All samples are from European ancestry.

Header line
SNP       SNP identifier
CHR       Chromosome
BP        Physical position (base-pair)
A1        Effect allele
A2        Reference allele
BETA      Effect size for binary phenotype (tranformed from BOLT-LMM BETA)
SE        Standard error
P         P-value

Note: Most of SNP names are in rsid format, some SNPs are in "chromsome:bp_A1_A2" format.




Citation: Zhu et al, Shared Genetics of Asthma and Mental Health Disorders: A Large-Scale Genome-Wide Cross-Trait Analysis. European Respiratory Journal, 2019 (PMID: 31619474)
